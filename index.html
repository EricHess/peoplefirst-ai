How it works

  1. 1) Audit your pain points

    We define where your team is overloaded and where you have skill gaps.

  2. 2) Set a specialist-light module stack

    Pick your first 2–3 modules: lead capture, follow-up, or reporting.

  3. 3) Human approval by default

    AI drafts and routes, but team ownership stays with people and accountability stays explicit.

  4. 4) Measure and optimize

    Weekly dashboard and coaching-style recommendations tell the team what to improve.
